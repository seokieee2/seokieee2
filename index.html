MY COLOR IS GRAY


Grey or gray is an intermediate color between black and white.
It is a neutral color or achromatic color, meaning literally that it is a color "without color",
because it can be composed of black and white.
The neutrality of gray gives us the ability to meditate and invisible.
Gray has the ability to show 'nothing' that other colors do not have.
